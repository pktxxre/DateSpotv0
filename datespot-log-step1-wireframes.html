Design decisions:

  • No current location dot. Users log after the date — they're home, not at the venue. The map shows past visit pins instead, which gives the map life without implying GPS relevance.
  • Past pins only. Red/amber/green colored pins showing historical visits. Empty map on first install; fills as the user logs more dates.
  • Three circle buttons, equal weight. No single action is forced as primary — users who want to search for a venue search, users who remember the street corner drop a pin, users who are still there use location.
  • Light map background. White/grey Apple Maps palette. Clean, legible, doesn't compete with the pins or the sheet.
